Community Leadership

Championing open data and youth-led environmental action in Malawi.

TEAM LEADER

OpenStreetMap Malawi

I serve as one of the three co-founding members of the board and currently lead the team at OpenStreetMap (OSM) Malawi. Since 2024, I have helped to organize State of the Map conferences and GIS conferences; notably, these were the first events of their kind to be held in Malawi.

ENVIRONMENTAL ADVOCACY

Youth for Environmental Development (YED)

I actively work with Youth for Environmental Development, an initiative focused on mobilizing young Malawians to take the lead in environmental conservation and sustainable development.

MENTOR

Technical Mentorship

I am dedicated to training the next generation of global leaders, with a specific focus on those with interests in science and community development. I believe in fostering talent that can bridge the gap between technical expertise and social impact.
