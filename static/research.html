Scientific Research & Inquiry

I am interested in answering questions that are important to doing things better. I address these questions through research I am currently pioneering, which leverages Remote Sensing and Machine Learning to optimize environmental and health interventions.

Beyond my personal research, I co-lead an informal geospatial unit, a collaborative group of geospatial researchers at the Malawi Liverpool Wellcome Research Programme dedicated to advancing spatial data science in the region.
